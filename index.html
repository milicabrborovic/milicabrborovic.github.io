I am Milica Brborović, an ambitious and creative web designer with two years of experience. My field of work is frontend design, which includes work in HTML, CSS, Boostrap, and jQuery (library of JavaScript). When I change photos and make some banners, I use Photoshop. Also, I'm working with some backend technologies such are PHP program languages and MySQL database. Besides, I'm operating with WordPress and use all its advantages. One of my activities is content writing for some blogs about a couple of things related to my profession. Also, I deal with digital marketing that includes banner designing, writing content for social networks, creating ads for Google and Facebook, and using tools for making email campaigns. Teaching is my particular passion. Due to that, I work as a lecturer of web design, digital marketing and an English teacher for foreigners. I'm highly interested in practicing and improving my knowledge as well as learning new methods in this creative branch. I am looking forward to getting in touch with you, therefore feel free to ask me anything you want to know.
